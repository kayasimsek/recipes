Semolina Pudding Recipe

This chocolate semolina pudding is a delectable dessert that is creamy, rich, and satisfying. The semolina adds a lovely texture to the pudding, while the chocolate gives it a decadent flavor that is balanced perfectly with the sweetness from the sugar. The addition of cardamom and saffron adds a subtle and delightful aroma and flavor to the pudding, making it a unique and aromatic treat. This dessert is perfect for any occasion and will leave your taste buds satisfied with its rich and creamy texture and delicious chocolatey taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3/4 cup semolina
  • 4 1/4 cups plant milk, plus extra if needed
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom
  • 1/8 teaspoon saffron
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a pan and add the semolina. Cook for 3-5 minutes.
  2. In a separate pot, simmer the plant milk, sugar, cardamom, saffron, and salt.
  3. Slowly add the semolina to the pot, whisking constantly.
  4. Once the mixture begins to thicken and bubble, remove from heat and let it rest.
  5. If needed, add warm plant milk until desired consistency is reached.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy!
